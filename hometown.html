Surakarta

Surakarta is a small city located in Central Java. It is known for it's rich cultural heritage and deep Javanese traditions. Surakarta is home to two historical palaces which is Kasunanan dan Mangkunegaran. The city's atmosphere is tend to be a slow living city with an "old city" and nostalgic vibes. Especially the largest traditional market in town, the one and only "Pasar Gede". It offers a unique culinary experience which no other market can't compare. It also offers a various type of local crafts, fruits, and plenty types of traditional snacks. With a mix of modern infrastructure and preserved cultural sites, the city has been turned to a charming destination for tourists. Especially, the night market which is held in Gatot Subroto Street every weekends that steals most people attention. Overall, Surakarta is a perfect town to live in, it has a perfect amount of destination, shopping malls, and offices. Although it's a small town, it almost has every thing that the people needed. Besides, it is not as busy as big cities like Jakarta and Surabaya, so definetely most people enjoys living in Solo since it's calm and comfortable.
